◆ LogonRealmOpcodes

enum LogonRealmOpcodes

Logon Realm communication.

These opcodes are used for logon/realm communication. All opcodes used by realm is marked as C (client). All opcodes used by logon is marked as S (server). Our logonserver is our communication server, the realm is our communication client.

Note
work in progress.
